Brakepad Akebono Project

(Nov 2021-Sept 2022)
This project is funded by LPDP and AKEBONO, our research collaboration involve some stakeholders (UGM,ATMI,AKEBONO). This research related to digital signal processing (AUDIO). Main of this research is to clasifying the object using sound as an input, in this case is brakepad.

There are 3 version of outcome. Prototype, MVP Product, Semi Mass Product. Prototype and MVP Product are develop in nov 2021 untul sept 2022. Semi Mass Product develop after sept 2022 until Feb 2023 and the semi Mass product will in HANNOVER MESSE exhibition on April 2023.

During development I do Collecting data for datasets, Analyze the data and modeling AI algorithm. Regardless, I involved in assembly machinery and testing system. Here some documentations :

IRV Project

(Nov 2022-Januari 2023)
This research related to digital signal processing (Vibration). Main of this research is to cluster the data using vibration as an input. There are types of defect identification in rail, such as corugation, iregularitation, many more. The purpose of this model is able to detect what defect is. We can monitor in Real-Time on website, so we know where the defect is. The system of IRV is integrated with IOT.
During development I do gathering data for datasets, Analyze the data, modeling AI algorithm and testing.
